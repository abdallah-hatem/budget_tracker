Getting started

1 · Create an account

Open the app and sign up with your email — you're in instantly.

2 · Add a transaction

Tap Add, then speak or type what you spent — e.g. "coffee 45 pounds" / "قهوة بـ٤٥ جنيه". Masareef detects the amount and category for you.

3 · Track your money

The dashboard shows your monthly income, spending, account balances, and a category breakdown.

Frequently asked questions

Does Masareef connect to my bank?

No. Masareef is a personal, track-only app. It never links to your bank account and never moves money — you stay fully in control.

How does voice entry work?

Speech recognition runs on your device. Tap the microphone, allow Microphone & Speech Recognition, and speak your expense in Arabic or English.

What is SMS auto-capture?

An optional iOS Shortcut that turns your bank's SMS alerts into pending transactions automatically. It's set up manually in the Shortcuts app and is completely optional — see the in-app guide under Settings.

How are accounts and balances calculated?

Add accounts like "Bank" or "Cash" with a starting balance. Each balance updates live as you add income and expenses. New transactions go to your default account; you can reassign any transaction when editing it.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is yours — no ads, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account or data?

You can delete individual transactions and accounts in the app. To delete your entire account and all data, email us and we'll remove it.
