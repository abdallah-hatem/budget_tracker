Delete Your Account

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This page explains how to delete your Masareef account (the personal budget-tracker app by Abdallah Hatem) and all of the data associated with it.

In short

Open the app → Settings → Delete account. Your account and all your data are permanently removed. You can also request deletion by email.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

  1. Open Masareef and sign in.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account is closed immediately and your data is scheduled for permanent deletion.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email support@masareef.app from the address on your account and ask us to delete it. We will verify ownership and remove your account and data.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of your data, including:

  • your account and email / sign-in identity;
  • all transactions (amounts, categories, notes, dates);
  • all accounts and balances, and any gold holdings;
  • your custom categories and keyword rules;
  • notification / push tokens.

What is kept, and for how long

We do not keep a copy of your personal data after deletion. Data is removed from our live database immediately and purged from encrypted backups within 30 days. We retain no transaction or notification content. Limited, non-identifying records may be kept only where required by law.
